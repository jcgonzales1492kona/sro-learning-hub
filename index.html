School safety takeaways

Keep roles clear

Safety, education, and discipline are different functions; agreements and policy should define the SRO role.

Use authorized training

For active-threat or CQB skills, use agency-approved, instructor-led training—not public tactical diagrams.

Verify before acting

Constitutional standards, school policy, and state law evolve. Confirm the current rule with qualified guidance.

Important: This is an educational reference. It does not provide legal advice or tactical direction. External links belong to their providers; verify all guidance with your agency, qualified legal counsel, and approved instructors.
